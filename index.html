Existing Conditions in Center City, Philadelphia, PA

1. Introduction

This interactive map will go through the existing conditions in Center City of Philadelphia County in Pennsylvania. You can do the following:

(1) Understand the constitution of Center City by census tracts and population.

(2) Check population, gender and race distribution in the census tract level.

(3) See the criminal incidents by selecting a census tract, a crime type, and a month.

(4) Get to know types of schools in each grade level.

(5) Filter Indego Bike Share stations by the numbers of available bikes and docks.

Center City is bounded by Pine Street to the south, the Delaware River to the east, the Schuylkill River to the west, and Vine Street to the north.

*Click on each census tract to see its name and population in 2014.
